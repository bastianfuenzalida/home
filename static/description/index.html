POS Home Delivery

Want to provide home delivery to customers? This module is designed to allow Home Delivery option to POS orders. With this module you can make customer get their desired products delivered to door-step. Also you can keep track on status of that order. It also allows you to know till what time customer should get products delivered to their door-step. Any special note can also be added while creating Order.

How to Use this module


First of all, in POS Configuration go to Point of Sale. You'll see "Home Delivery" option in features menu. Just select it.


After that, go to Available Payment Methods, where you can see cash. In cash mode, go to Point of Sale and select Use as "Home Delivery".


Inside POS Order

Now, You are ready to place an Order. It's same as earlier. Select customer, products with quantity, and other details.

Once Order is ready to be paid, hit "Home Delivery" button, which will open a "Home Delivery Order" form. Here you will see two Delivery option: Default Selected Customer Address OR Shipped to Different Address


As you click "Create" button, a small dialogue will open that will give you confirmation that "Home Delivery" order is generated.


If you want to order at different address than tick "Ship to different Address" and fill up the form and then click "Create" button.


As you click "Create" button, a small dialogue will open that will give you confirmation that "Home Delivery" order is generated.


Back in Home Delivery

Here all orders made with Home Delivery option are Shown along with status.



Detailed order is displayed here. It contains Customer details, items that are to be delivered and stat of the order.


In Customer delivery address is created.


After Order is created, Go ahead and make payment. Once payment is validated, Work is almost done. There is one thing to keep in mind that we'll see in few moments.


Once payment is done, receipt of that oder will be generated automatically.


All the details of particular orders along with its change in state can be seen in particular order. Also from here bank statement that is generated can be accessed.


A journal entry after payment of order is registered.


A thing that should be kept in mind.

Once order for Home Delivery is created, If state of order is changed to Paid without validating payment. An error message will be shown just like shown in below image. To avoid this Error. Once order is created, Validate Payment from POS. After that we can change State of Order to Paid.
